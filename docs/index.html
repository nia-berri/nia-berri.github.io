01 — About

Background

I come to data science and statistics by way of history. Years spent studying events, art and architecture taught me to ask the important question ... `what are we not seeing`?. This habit carried me into data science and statistics. Instict, paired with a genuine interest now validates how i view the world.

I have recently completed a graduate certificate in Data Science from American University, where I am still completing the MSc in Data Science. Here my emphasis lie in AI, but also in public health. This interest stems from my historical interest in medicine and art during the Renaissance era in Europe, where I am currently researching the link between art and medical procedures under the Catholic Church's dominance.
