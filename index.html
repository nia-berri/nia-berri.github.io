01 — About

Background

I'm a data analyst completing an MS Graduate Certificate in Data Science, with an emphasis on AI and public health, at American University. My background is in adult social care and public-sector operations, which is where the interest in equity-focused analysis started — I kept noticing which populations the data made visible, and which it didn't.

My recent work has centred on health insurance coverage risk and US mortality disparities, using the kind of federal survey and surveillance data that shapes real policy. I'm most interested in roles where statistical rigor and a public-health lens meet — data science, applied statistics, or analyst positions with a genuine stake in the outcome.
